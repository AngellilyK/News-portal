Alpine walk for extreme lovers

The Red Bull X-Alps is an adventure race that takes participants through five countries to cross the Alps on foot and in a paraglider. According to the rules, participants have 12 days to cross the Alps on foot and by paraglider along a route consisting of several control points, which change from race to race. It is a true test of strength, endurance and skill for even the most experienced athletes.

Travellers stuck on the road to the Alps

Heavy snowfall has left travellers on their way to resorts in the Alps stranded. Motorists spent the night in makeshift shelters after the roads became impassable due to snow. The rest of the tourists who had just arrived at the airport were also forced to sleep in shelters. One skier was killed in an avalanche.The avalanche danger level at the airport has reached 4 out of 5 points.

Hiking suspended due to worsening weather

The avalanche danger level is at level 4 in most of the Alps after almost a metre of snow fell in 24 hours. The problem is not the depth of snow, but the fact that more than a metre has fallen in such a short time. The weather forecast promises heavy snowfall over the next few days. The atmosphere in these areas is calm this year, as the communes are already used to such winter situations.
