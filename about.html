Our Mission

The aim of this news portal is to provide our readers with up-to-date and verified information. At the moment, there are many who wish to spend their holiday in the numerous ski resorts of the Alps. Thus, by visiting our website and finding out all the latest news, you will be able to work out your holiday plan in more detail, taking into account all the events, recommendations, those who have already been on one hike or another, and be prepared for any incidents.

Alps

The Alps are the highest and longest mountain range among the systems that lie entirely in Europe. The Alps cover eight countries: France, Monaco, Italy, Switzerland, Germany, Austria, Liechtenstein and Slovenia. The total length of the Alpine arc is about 1200 km. The highest peak of the Alps is Mount Mont Blanc, 4,810 metres above sea level, located on the border between France and Italy. The Alps are an international centre for mountaineering, skiing and tourism.
